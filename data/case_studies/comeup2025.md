
# **COMEUP 2025 / DEC 10-12, 2025 @COEX, Seoul, Korea**  

## **Introduction**  
**COMEUP 2025** is the largest global startup festival in Korea, bringing together startups, investors, corporations, and key players in the entrepreneurial ecosystem from around the world to explore technological innovation and opportunities for collaboration. Launched in 2019, the event has continued to grow each year and now serves as a key platform connecting Korean startups with global investors and companies.

The 2025 edition will be held under the theme “Recode the Future.” It highlights the role of startups in redefining the future beyond industry and national boundaries through technological innovation and entrepreneurship. The program includes conferences, startup exhibitions, IR pitching sessions, investor meetings, and open innovation programs. Various sessions will focus on technology, global expansion, and the startup ecosystem.

## **Accomodation**  
Accommodation was arranged with 2 five-star hotels, 3 four-star hotels, and 3 three-star hotels located near COEX, the event venue.
Successfully provided a range of accommodation options with different price points and preferred locations. 

---

## **Results Achieved**  
- **Higher attendee satisfaction:**
	Providing official accommodation information encouraged participants to engage more actively in the event.
- **Discounted hotel rates:**
	Accommodation was offered at approximately 10% lower than OTA prices, providing better value for attendees.
- **Reduced operational workload:**
	Event organizers did not need to manage accommodation-related tasks, as all booking operations were handled for them.

---

## **Conclusion**  
We simplified the accommodation management for COMEUP organizers by providing a dedicated hotel booking platform, handling group and VIP reservations, and supporting hotel negotiations and operational coordination.
Through a booking-based revenue-sharing model, the organizers were able to generate additional income while attendees benefited from a more convenient booking experience and competitively priced hotel options.
