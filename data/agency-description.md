**RIAD Corporation** empowers travel professionals and event organizers to manage hotel sourcing and accommodation operations more efficiently. Its platform,**yeyak**, helps travel teams generate structured, branded hotel proposals within minutes, enabling agencies to present professional comparisons, respond to client requests faster, and improve their chances of winning deals.

RIAD’s approach goes beyond simple hotel booking by supporting the entire proposal and operational workflow. Through yeyak, users can discover hotels using natural-language search, source rates through direct requests or instant supply options, generate fully branded proposals, and publish dedicated booking pages for each project. This allows partners to retain full ownership of their customer relationships, pricing strategies, and brand experience. For event organizers, Ria event provides a full-service event housing solution that manages curated hotel options, participant bookings, VIP reservations, and attendee support. The model is designed to reduce operational risk by offering room block management without traditional attrition penalties.

RIAD focuses on delivering measurable operational efficiency and commercial impact. The **yeyak** platform significantly reduces the time required to create hotel proposals while allowing agencies to handle more opportunities and maintain complete brand control over proposals and booking pages. Meanwhile, Ria event enables organizers to launch dedicated event booking platforms quickly, minimize upfront operational burden, and share revenue generated from completed reservations. Together, these solutions aim to streamline the event accommodation process while helping partners scale their business more effectively.
